About The Galaxy

The Universe Awaits

The observable universe spans approximately 93 billion light-years in diameter, containing over two trillion galaxies, each housing hundreds of billions of stars. Our Milky Way alone stretches 100,000 light-years across.

From violent stellar nurseries to the crushing gravity of black holes, the cosmos is a tapestry of wonders billions of years in the making. Every point of light tells an ancient story.
